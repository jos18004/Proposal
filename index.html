Overview

Purpose

I am designing a personal portfolio website to showcase my skills, educational achievements, personal work, and resume professionally. This will act as my online identity and electronic resume to share with potential employers, mentors, and internship coordinators.

Audience

The audience includes university students, teachers, recruiters, and internship coordinators interested in learning about my experience. The site will be clean, easy to navigate, and professional.
